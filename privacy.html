Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

1. Introduction

SoundProfit Market ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register an account, we collect:

  • Username and email address
  • Password (encrypted and hashed)
  • Profile information (avatar, bio)
  • Ethereum wallet address (for payments)

2.2 Transaction Data

For purchases and sales, we record:

  • Transaction amounts and timestamps
  • Blockchain transaction hashes
  • Song purchases and downloads
  • Payment evidence (screenshots, if provided)

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Song plays and interactions
  • Referral sources

2.4 Uploaded Content

Artists upload:

  • Music files (audio)
  • Metadata (title, genre, ISRC codes)
  • Cover art images

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Process transactions, enable downloads, manage accounts
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, enhance features
  • Communication: Send transaction confirmations, platform updates, support responses
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce terms
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with regulations, respond to legal requests

4. Blockchain and Cryptocurrency

Important: Blockchain transactions are public and permanent. When you make a purchase:

  • Your wallet address is visible on the blockchain
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps are public
  • This data cannot be deleted or modified
  • We do not control blockchain data

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers:

  • Cloud hosting (AWS, Render.com)
  • Database services (PostgreSQL)
  • Email services (for notifications)
  • Analytics tools (to improve the platform)

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Ensure user safety

5.4 Business Transfers

If SoundProfit Market is acquired or merged, user data may be transferred to the new entity.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Passwords are hashed with bcrypt
  • HTTPS: All communications are encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Files stored on secure servers
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to data
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute-force attacks

Note: No system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.

7. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain data processing
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@soundprofit.market

8. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active Accounts: As long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Up to 90 days for backup purposes
  • Transaction Records: 7 years for legal and tax compliance
  • Blockchain Data: Permanent (cannot be deleted)

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage to improve the platform
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites (e.g., blockchain explorers, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies.

11. Children's Privacy

SoundProfit Market is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

12. International Users

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your residence. By using the platform, you consent to this transfer.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced on the platform. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
